Meet the authors

Check out the latest titles from these Harvard authors.

Certainly! Claire Messud is an American writer and novelist. She has authored several acclaimed novels, including “The Emperor’s Children” and “The Woman.”

Certainly! Archon Fung is the Winthrop Laflin McCormack Professor of Citizenship and Self-Government at the Harvard Kennedy School.

Certainly! Tiya Miles is an award-winning author, historian, and Professor at Harvard University. She holds the Michael Garvey Professor of History position.

Certainly! Matthew Ichihashi Potts is the Plummer Professor of Christian Morals at Harvard Divinity School and serves as the Pusey Minister in the Harvard University.

Certainly! Emiliana Vegas is a U.S.-based policy analyst, academic, and author. She holds the position of Professor of Practice at the Graduate School of Education.

Certainly! Dr. Irvin Scott is a learner, leader, speaker, author, philanthropist, and Harvard educator. He joined the Harvard Graduate School of Education in 2016.
